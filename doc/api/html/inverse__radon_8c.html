Detailed Description

NFFT-based discrete inverse Radon transform.

Computes the inverse of the discrete Radon transform

given at the points of the polar or linogram grid and where are the weights of the Dirichlet- or Fejer-kernel by 1D-FFTs and the 2D-iNFFT.
